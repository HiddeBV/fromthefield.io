Our Mission

From The Field is a technical podcast created by consultants, for consultants. We bring you authentic conversations about the real challenges and solutions in modern technology consulting.

Whether you're a developer, architect, DevOps engineer, or tech leader, our episodes dive deep into the topics that matter: cloud architecture, platform engineering, Kubernetes, site reliability, and the unique dynamics of consulting work.

We believe in sharing practical knowledge from the field—not just theory, but real-world experiences, lessons learned, and honest discussions about what works (and what doesn't) in enterprise technology.

Technical Depth

We don't shy away from complex topics. Each episode goes deep into technical challenges and architectural decisions.

Real Experiences

Honest Conversations

No marketing fluff. We discuss real challenges, trade-offs, and the realities of working in tech consulting.
