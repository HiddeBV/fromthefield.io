Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days. For guest inquiries, we may take a bit longer as we review your proposal and discuss with the team.

What makes a good guest topic?

The best topics combine technical depth with practical insights. We love stories about real challenges, architectural decisions, and lessons learned from the field.

Do you accept sponsorships?

We're selective about sponsorships to maintain the podcast's integrity. Use the contact form with "Other" as the topic if you'd like to discuss partnership opportunities.

Can I suggest an episode topic?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our listeners about topics they'd like us to cover. Select "Feedback" in the contact form and share your ideas.
